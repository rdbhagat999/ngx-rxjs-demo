Actions

When we zip color$ and logo$, we expect to receive an array of 2 items during subscribe, the first item is color and the second is logo (follow their orders in zip function). zip operator can accept more than 2 observables - no matter how many observables, they must all wait for each other, no man left behind!

I call combineLatest operator the independent operator. They are independent and donâ€™t wait for each other. Either the color or logo value change will trigger the log.

I call withLatestFrom operator the primary/secondary operator. At first, the primary must meet the secondary. After that, the primary will take the lead, giving command. The secondary will follow.

forkJoin is a special case of combineLatest. It will wait for all observables to complete.
